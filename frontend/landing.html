The process

Matching built on
intention, not impulse

Every match on Hassabe passes through two rounds of deep AI compatibility analysis before you're introduced. This is matchmaking, not browsing.

Step 01
01

Build Your Profile

A guided 8-step onboarding captures who you are — your faith, heritage, profession, family values, and relationship goals.

Step 02
02

Round One Questionnaire

30 questions across 8 dimensions: values, culture, religion, communication, lifestyle, family, goals, and deal-breakers. ~12 minutes.

Step 03
03

AI Compatibility Match

Semantic embedding models analyze your responses. Only pairs scoring above 72% compatibility are surfaced as potential matches.

Step 04
04

Round Two — Deeper

When a strong match is found, both complete a second questionnaire: marriage timeline, finances, family roles, emotional readiness.

Step 05
05

Final AI Evaluation

Combined scores from both rounds are re-evaluated. If both pass the threshold, the match is confirmed with a compatibility summary.

Step 06
06

Begin Your Conversation

Messaging unlocks with AI-generated icebreakers rooted in your shared values. One conversation. Full attention.

Our AI uses semantic embedding models to understand the meaning behind your questionnaire responses — not just checkbox matching. It scores compatibility at the level of values and worldview.
